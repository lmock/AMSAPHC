The UC Berkeley American Medical Student Association presents:

Inspiring the Healthcare Leaders of Tomorrow

8th Annual UC Berkeley Pre-Health Conference, 2015

Saturday, February 7th, 8:30 AM - 5:00 PM

University of California, Berkeley

330 Sutardja Dai Hall

Attire: Business Casual

The University of California, Berkeley American Medical Student Association (AMSA) is proud to announce our 8th Annual Pre-Health Conference. The conference will be hosted on the UC Berkeley campus on Saturday, February 7th, 2015 in Sutardja Dai Hall.

This conference is a resource for pre-health students looking to gather information about succeeding in the health professions, network with peers who are pursuing similar career goals, and find mentors to guide them through the process of pursuing a career in the healthcare arena.

We will kick off our conference with a keynote speech by our physician mentor, Dr. Igor Mitrovic. Dr. Mitrovic is a professor of physiology, as well as the Director of Professional School Education at the University of California, San Francisco. He is a valued member of the AMSA family, and has been a great source of inspiration to many premedical students here at UC Berkeley.

The conference will also feature a variety of speaker panels which will be focused on different aspects of healthcare. You will have the opportunity to hear from some of the leading healthcare professionals of today, coming from all sorts of unique professional and educational backgrounds -- MD, DO, PhD, MPH, MBA, RN, and many combinations in between. The following is a list of a few of the professional panels we will be having this year:

Physician Panel

Life as a Surgeon

Combing Research & Medicine

D.O. vs M.D.

The Future of Healthcare: A Health Tech Panel

Tomorrow's Biggest Challenges in Public Health

Medical Student Panel

Nursing Panel

Pharmacy Panel

Suture Clinic

+ More!

The conference will also hold a variety of technical workshops specific for different stages of the pre-medical track. Several workshops will focus on practical advice for applying to medical school, preparing for the MCAT, and preparing for interviews. We will also be hosting a few suture clinic workshops and a graduate school fair, consisting of representatives from a variety of professional schools and health-centered organizations.

If you have attended our conference in the past, we hope to welcome you back as we will be continuing to offer some of our most popular workshops/panels, as well as introducing new panels with a variety of new speakers. We would also like to welcome new attendees in joining hundreds of passionate pre-health students in attaining a wealth of information and skills needed to succeed as a future healthcare professional.

Breakfast and lunch will be provided.
